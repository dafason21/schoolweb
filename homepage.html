Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

What is the admission process?

Admission to our school is based on national exam results (KCPE/O-level) and interview. Students complete an application form, submit their exam results, and attend an entrance interview. Admission is done on a merit basis, with consideration for various factors.

What is the school fees structure?

School fees vary based on the level and boarding status. Tuition includes all learning materials, laboratory fees, and access to facilities. We offer payment plans and financial assistance for eligible students. Contact the school office for detailed fee structure.

What are the school hours?

Regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 4:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Classes commence with morning assembly and include break times. Boarding students have supervised study times in the evening. Saturday is used for co-curricular activities and sports.

Are there boarding facilities?

Yes, we offer both day and boarding options. Our boarding facilities are well-maintained with dormitories, dining hall, safe water supply, and 24-hour security. Boarding students receive care from qualified housemasters and matrons who ensure their comfort and discipline.

What support is available for exam preparation?

We provide comprehensive exam preparation support including past papers, intensive revision classes before national exams, career guidance, and counseling. Teachers conduct regular assessments to monitor student progress and provide targeted support for struggling students.
